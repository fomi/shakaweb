The School

First lesson, life time addiction

The Shaka Method

Tailored to You

Whether it's your very first time on a board or you're chasing your next move, our lessons are tailored to you. Our instructors follow every student from a support boat, so you're never alone on the water, at any level, from absolute beginner to advanced.

Giorgio, Mitch and Henri are VDWS-certified instructors with over 15 years of teaching experience. They know how to read the conditions, read the rider, and get you progressing fast while having fun.

The bay of Costa Calma is the perfect place to learn: flat, shallow water protected from the wind. Safe, calm and forgiving. Perfect for kids too.

Windsurf

The original board sport. Feel the wind, ride the water, and experience pure freedom. Perfect for all ages, from your first tack to planing at full speed.

Wingfoil
